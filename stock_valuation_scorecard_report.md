**4. Zero Debt, Capital-Light:** HDFCAMC does not need to borrow or raise capital to grow. All growth is self-funded through internal cash generation. This means no dilution, no interest costs, and all profits belong to shareholders.

**5. Valuation Is Fair:** At 35.6x PE with 27%+ earnings growth and 39% ROCE, the PEG of ~1.3 is reasonable. You are paying fair value for an A+ quality business.

**Risks:** Regulatory risk (SEBI fee cuts), market downturn reducing AUM, competition from passive/direct plans.

**Verdict: STRONG BUY — Deploy all tranches. This is a core portfolio holding.**
